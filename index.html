FAQ

What is quantum tech?

It's a range of technologies, including quantum computing, quantum communication, and quantum sensing. Quantum computing is a type of computing that uses quantum-mechanical phenomena, such as superposition and entanglement, to perform certain calculations faster.

Will quantum computing break blockchain?

Eventually, yes‚Äîbut the timeline matters. Quantum computers capable of breaking current cryptographic signatures will likely arrive in the coming years. At Quredge, quantum researchers and web3 builders collaborate on understanding the threat landscape and building solutions.

What is post-quantum cryptography?

PQC uses mathematical problems believed to resist quantum attacks‚Äîlattice problems, multivariate polynomials. Standards have been finalized, and the conversation now shifts to implementation. The residency brings together those who understand the theory with those building the infrastructure. But we aim for more!

What is distributed quantum computing?

Rather than single quantum processors, distributed networks of quantum resources working together. Web3's decentralized coordination models offer unexpected frameworks for quantum resource sharing, privacy-preserving computation, and new governance structures.

Do I need deep technical background?

No: you need to bring curiosity and humilty. We expect quantum researchers with PhD level, to Web3 builders developing blockchain applications, to thinkers and creativesbring disruptive conceptual frameworks. The magic happens when different types of expertise converge‚Äîcuriosity matters more than credentials!
