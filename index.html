FAQ

What is quantum technology?

A range of technologies leveraging quantum mechanics: quantum computing (faster calculations via superposition/entanglement), quantum sensing (ultra-precise measurements for biotech, neurotech), and quantum communication (unhackable networks). The "second quantum revolution" is happening now.

How does quantum connect to AI?

Quantum machine learning could exponentially accelerate certain AI tasks. Conversely, AI helps discover better quantum algorithms. At the frontier: quantum approaches to AI alignment and safety research. This intersection is one of Quredge's core tracks.

What's quantum sensing and why does it matter?

Quantum sensors detect incredibly small signals‚Äîenabling breakthroughs in brain imaging (neurotech), drug discovery (biotech), materials science, and navigation. Often overlooked compared to computing, but closer to real-world impact.

How does quantum relate to consciousness research?

The measurement problem, observer effects, and foundational questions about reality. Controversial but fascinating territory where physics meets philosophy. Edge's Consciousness Week provides rigorous yet open-minded exploration space.

Do I need a deep technical background?

Curiosity and humility matter most. We welcome quantum researchers (PhD-level), AI/neurotech/biotech builders, and creative thinkers with disruptive frameworks. Breakthroughs emerge at disciplinary boundaries‚Äîdiverse expertise creates unexpected collisions.

How does Quredge connect to other Edge residencies?

Direct overlaps with d/acc (decentralized tech), Consciousness at the Edge, Health & Bio, Neurotech Summit, and Hard Tech tracks. You'll collide with 1000+ builders across 25+ residencies‚Äîquantum as a lens on multiple frontier domains. See all Edge Esmeralda tracks

What outcomes can I expect?

Depends on your goals: learn something new, spark curiosity, start collaborative papers, build code repos, or fund your startup. Past Edge cohorts: $250M+ raised by alumni, 8+ papers published, 100+ hires placed. Lasting collaborations that continue years later.
